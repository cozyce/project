Eureka! Learn About CA

Located in the Western United States, California lays between Oregon, Nevada, Arizona and the Pacific Coast. California is the US state that has the largest population, almost 9,000,000 more than the second largest US state, Texas.

California was admitted to the Union on September 9th, 1950

California is known for it's beautiful beaches for surfing, it's mountains for hiking, and it's National Parks for adventuring. The state slogan is Eureka! which refers to the words said when gold was found
